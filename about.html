Snoweria Zhang

Hi! My name is Snoweria. I am an artist + designer who enjoys mathematics, earrings, and tea.

I'm based in New York currently but have lived and worked in many cities around the world. As a result, I am a teensy confused culturally. In 2017, my proposal to hybridize a waste-to-energy plant with a mosque was awarded an Architizer A+ Jury Award. Most recently, my design work was featured on the cover of Nature.

I have a Master of Architecture and a Bachelor of Arts in Mathematics, both from Harvard University. I previouly spent most of my time thinking about future cities at the Senseable City Lab at MIT. Currently, I am the Urban Innovation Architect at KPF and an Ajunct Assistant Professor at Columbia University Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation.

Below is a list of talks I've given and chapters I've written, in case you really want to know what I think of technology and cities.
