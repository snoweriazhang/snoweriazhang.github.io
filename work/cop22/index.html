ABOUT THE PROJECT

Work completed during internship at Oualalou + Choi, Paris.

COP (Conference of the Parties) 22 took place in Marrakech, Morocco in November, 2016. Oualalou + Choi won the competition to design the master plan as well as the various architectural elements for the conference. During the summer internship, I was responsible for visualizing the design for conference publications.
