ABOUT THE PROJECT

Written by Jean Genet
Directed by Matthew C. Stone
Set design by Elizabeth Mak and Snoweria Zhang

This French play is set in a brothel in an unnamed city that is experiencing revolutionary uprising in its streets. The design captured the erotic energy as well as the political upheavals the characters face. Larger-than-life paintings of sensuous, nude women flanked the triptychlike doors of the brothel, while red velvet walls depicted the visitorsâ€™ innermost fantasies. During the revolution scenes, over 600 red bricks were raucously punched out of the structure, leaving a bare steel frame that underlined the destruction that was left behind.
