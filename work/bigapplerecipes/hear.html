New Yorkers love to complain about how loud the city is. In fact, more than 4 million 311 noise complaints were made between 2010 and 2019. The HEAR recipe below takes the 15 sounds that New Yorkers complain about the most and composes them into a recipe for a 1-minute performance. The world premiere features Canadian soprano Rose Hegele. Recreate the soundscape of city living from your home with #bigapplerecipes.
